How It Works

Set it up once. BuddyBlink takes care of the rest quietly in the background.

1

Create a schedule

Choose how often you'd like to check in — once a day, every few hours, whatever suits your routine.

2

Add your contacts

Choose who should be notified and how — via Telegram or email. They don't need to install anything.

3

That's it

If you miss a check-in, BuddyBlink sends your contacts a message on your behalf. If you don't miss one, nothing happens. Simple.
